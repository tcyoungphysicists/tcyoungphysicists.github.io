Welcome to TCYP

The official student wing of the Tri-Chandra Research Group

Tri-Chandra Young Physicists (TCYP) is a dynamic student organization founded on April 28, 2025, under the Tri-Chandra Research Group at Tri-Chandra Multiple Campus, Tribhuvan University, Kathmandu. TCYP is dedicated to fostering a passion for physics and scientific research among students in Nepal. As a community of enthusiastic young physicists, we aim to promote collaboration, critical thinking, and active learning. We provide a platform for budding physicists to connect, learn, and grow together through academic events, outreach programs, and global engagement.

Research Group

We are proudly connected to the Tri-Chandra Research Group, working on cutting-edge physics research projects and collaborations.

International Affiliation

As a member of the International Association of Physics Students (IAPS), we connect our members to a global network of physics students.

Community

Join our vibrant community of like-minded individuals passionate about understanding the fundamental laws of the universe.
